About Me

Learn more about my journey, mindset, passion for cybersecurity, and my mission to spread ethical hacking knowledge in a positive and legal way.

My Journey in Cybersecurity

My name is Rishabh, and I started my journey in cybersecurity with a strong curiosity about how the digital world works behind the scenes. I began learning through mobile platforms, experimenting with Termux, Linux environments, and basic networking concepts. Over time, I developed a deep interest in ethical hacking, cybersecurity awareness, and how systems can be protected from real-world threats.

I believe learning security is not about breaking systems, but about understanding them deeply so that we can make technology safer for everyone. My journey is focused on legal, ethical, and responsible cybersecurity practices.

Skills & Areas of Interest

I actively work and practice in multiple domains of cybersecurity and technology, including:

• Ethical Hacking Fundamentals
• Mobile Penetration Testing (For Educational Labs Only)
• Termux & Linux Based Tools
• OSINT (Open Source Intelligence)
• Networking Basics & Web Security Concepts
• Bug Bounty Learning Platforms (HTB, TryHackMe, Labs)
• Content Creation for Tech & Cybersecurity Awareness

Along with technical skills, I am also passionate about teaching, simplifying complex topics, and creating valuable content for beginners who want to enter the cybersecurity field.

My Vision

My long-term goal is to become a skilled cybersecurity professional and security researcher who can help organizations and individuals stay safe online. I aim to build a strong learning community where students and beginners can learn cybersecurity in a simple, ethical, and legal way without confusion.

Through my portfolio, social media, and content, I want to inspire more people to enter the world of cybersecurity with the right mindset and responsible approach.
